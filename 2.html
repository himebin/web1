Album

South Korean singer-songwriter IU has released five full-length studio albums, nine extended plays (EPs), 47 singles (including 19 as featured artist), five single albums, two remake albums and two compilation albums. In addition, the singer has collaborations and soundtracks for television series, films, and online games (including unreleased soundtracks).
